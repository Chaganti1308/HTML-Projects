Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all purpose floor
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semi sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF or 170oC
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the melted butter,white sugar, and brown sugar until well blended.
  3. Beat the vanilla extract and eggs until light and creamy.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl.Gradually stir into butter and sugar mixture.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips by hand using a wooden spoon.
  6. Drop cookie dough by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets
  7. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are lightly browned.
  8. Cool on baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.

Notes

For softer cookies, reduce baking time slightly.For crispier cookies, increase baking time slowly.Enjoy your homemade chocolate chip cookies with a glass of milk!
